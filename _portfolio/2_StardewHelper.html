--- title: "Stardew Helper" excerpt: "A Stardew Valley companion website that helps players optimize their in-game decisions.
" collection: portfolio --- This project explores gameplay data from Stardew Valley through an interactive multi-page website. Built with JavaScript, D3.js, Leaflet.js, HTML, and CSS, it transforms complex datasets into visualizations that help players optimize their in-game decisions.

Key Features

  • Fish Visualization: Interactive map with filters and radar charts showing fish availability, profitability by time, weather, and season.
  • Recipe Visualization: Force-directed graph and scatterplot linking recipes to ingredients, with filters for categories and attributes.
  • Crop Visualization: Interactive bar and slope charts tracking profitability trends across days and seasons.

Data Processing

  • Scraped and cleaned datasets from Stardew Valley Wiki and Kaggle.
  • Automated extraction and transformation using Google Sheets scripts and Python.
  • Merged multi-dimensional data (fish probabilities, crop growth cycles, recipe ingredients).

Overall, this website allows the user to know:
  • Optimal fishing times and locations.
  • Trade-offs between recipe cost and profitability.
  • Seasonal crop strategies for maximizing profit.
