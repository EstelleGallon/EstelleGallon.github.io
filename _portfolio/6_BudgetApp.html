" collection: portfolio --- This project involved the end-to-end development of a financial management application, from initial user research to prototyping and a formal usability evaluation. The goal was to create a tool that helps users gain a clear overview of their income and expenses. The app was developped using Android Studio.

Key Features

  • Customizable Transaction Management: Organize income, spending, and savings with fully customizable categories.
  • Goal-Oriented Budgeting: Set and track specific saving goals and category-specific spending limits.
  • Visual Financial Overview: Visualize expenses by category and track financial progress over time via an integrated calendar.

Methodology & Process

  • User Needs Assessment: Conducted a quantitative survey to identify core features, preferred devices, and pain points with existing tools.
  • Competitive Analysis: Analyzed feedback on tools like Revolut, Boursorama, and Notion to understand what to keep and what to avoid.
  • Iterative Prototyping & Evaluation: Built a functional prototype and conducted a three-phase usability study:
    • Unstructured Exploration: Observed natural user interaction to identify intuitive features and unanticipated pain points.
    • Structured Task Completion: Quantified performance on 19 core tasks, measuring error rates and efficiency.
    • Post-Task Interview: Gathered qualitative feedback on task difficulty and user frustration.
